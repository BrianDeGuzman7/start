About me

I was born and raised in the suburbs of Northern California. Every time I tell people that I am from Tracy, I am met with pondering looks. It has grown greatly since my younger years however, I am proud that I came from this little suburban town.

My hard working work ethic and determined personality come from my parents. They have greatly influenced my life and shaped me into the man I am today. I love them a lot and hope to make them proud everyday.

University of California, Santa Cruz

I grew up in the suburbs but I matured greatly at my time at University of California, Santa Cruz. In 2019, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Bachelor of Arts in Business Management Economics.
